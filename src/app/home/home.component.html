Why Orange County Music School?

Simple/Easy

We know your schedule is packed. Our teachers come to you making life that much easier. Music lessons can also be in studio, or you can study with one of our amazing NYC teachers from anywhere in the country with an on-line lesson.

Skilled Teachers

The brightest, coolest, most talented music teachers in NYC, inspiring students to love, play and create music. They will come up with a learning plan to meet the needs of any student no matter what age or skill level.

SERVICE

Music To Your Home is a family owned company here 24/7 to get you set up and learning. Monthly billing and easy payment options are available. Need help finding an instrument? We’ll assist you in choosing all instruments and materials so you can focus on the lessons.

GUARANTEE

We’re so confident in our service that we promise if your teacher isn’t the perfect fit we will match you with another instructor or refund any unused payments. You’re never tied into a contract and there are no hidden registration fees!
